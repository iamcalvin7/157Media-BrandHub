### Why Choose Our Hop-On Hop-Off Experience?

##### Fast & Convenient Travel to Gozo

Reach Gozo quickly and comfortably from multiple locations in Malta. You can depart from:

- **Valletta’s Grand Harbour** by fast ferry (approx. 45 minutes)
- **Sliema**, with a scenic coastal route via Bugibba
- **Bugibba**, with direct departures to Gozo

When you arrive at **Mgarr Harbour**, simply board the **Gozo Hop-On Hop-Off bus** and begin exploring.

##### Discover Gozo at Your Own Pace

Our Hop-On Hop-Off bus tour allows you to explore **Gozo’s top attractions** without worrying about transport.

- Victoria and the historic **Citadel**
- Xlendi Bay
- Dwejra Bay
- Marsalforn
- Ramla Bay

Hop off to explore, enjoy lunch, take photos, or relax by the sea before continuing your journey.

##### Flexible All-Day Bus Ticket

Your **Hop-On Hop-Off bus ticket in Gozo** is valid for the entire day.

Buses follow a fixed timetable and stop at the island’s most popular landmarks. Simply hop on or off whenever you like and continue your adventure when the next **GREEN bus** arrives.

##### Perfect for Families

Our Gozo tour is ideal for families visiting Malta.

Children aged **4–10 enjoy reduced ticket prices**, making this a great option for discovering Gozo together.

### Ferry Departures & Locations

##### Valletta to Gozo Fast Ferry

Depart from the **Gozo Fast Ferry Terminal at Ta’ Liesse in Valletta’s Grand Harbour.**.

If you’re in Valletta’s city centre, the **Barrakka Lift** provides quick and easy access to the ferry terminal.

##### Sliema to Gozo (via Bugibba)

Visitors staying in **Sliema or St. Julian’s** can board the ferry in Sliema. The route travels along Malta’s beautiful northern coast and stops in **Bugibba** before continuing to Gozo.

This option makes travelling to Gozo convenient for travellers staying in Malta’s main tourist areas.

##### Bugibba to Gozo

Direct departures from **Bugibba** provide one of the easiest ways to travel to Gozo, especially for visitors staying in **St Paul’s Bay, Qawra, or Mellieha.**

The ferry crossing offers scenic coastal views before arriving at **Mgarr Harbour in Gozo.**

##### Return from Gozo

Return crossings depart from **Mgarr Harbour**, allowing you to easily match your return journey with the ferry timetable.

Whether you’re planning a **half-day Gozo trip** or a **full-day island adventure**, this experience fits perfectly into your Malta holiday itinerary.
